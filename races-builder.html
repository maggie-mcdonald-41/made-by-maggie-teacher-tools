What This Tool Supports

Built for strong constructed responses, without giving students the answer:

  • RACES step-by-step flow: Restate → Answer → Cite → Explain → Summarize.
  • Sentence starter support: Helps students begin each section confidently.
  • Evidence reminders: Prompts students to pull the strongest quote, not the longest.
  • Explanation scaffolds: Reinforces evidence → reasoning connections.
  • Editable final response view: Students can revise while keeping structure.
  • Read-aloud accessibility: Supports struggling readers and writers.
  • Optional voice selection: Lets students choose a comfortable reader voice (if enabled).
  • Dyslexia-friendly font option: Improves readability.
  • Dark mode: Reduces eye strain.
  • Confetti celebrations: Motivates students through progress milestones.
  • Local autosave: Students can safely work across days.
  • Optional Drive + Docs export: Save to Google Drive and export to Google Docs (if enabled).

How It Works in Class

Simple routine teachers can use with any text:

  • Choose any passage + question: Use your curriculum text, an assessment passage, or a short article.
  • Model once, then release: Complete one response together, then students work independently.
  • Coach without giving answers: Sentence starters guide structure while students supply ideas and evidence.
  • Revise with the checklist: Students review their final response and strengthen clarity and evidence.
  • Save + continue later: Local autosave supports multi-day writing without lost work.

Standards Alignment

Designed to reinforce evidence-based writing and reading response routines aligned to common ELA expectations. Works across grade levels because teachers supply the text and prompt.

Common alignment targets:

  • Text evidence: Cite relevant evidence to support analysis and responses.
  • Written response structure: Clear restatement, direct answer, explanation, and wrap-up.
  • Language and conventions: Clarity, grammar, and academic tone during revision.
