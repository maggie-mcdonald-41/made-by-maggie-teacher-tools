🎒 Designed with Students & Teachers in Mind

🧠 Smart Writing Flow

From prompt to polished draft, this organizer helps students build real essays—while automatically connecting their ideas across sections:

  • ✨ Dynamic Thesis Builder: Students build a strong thesis step by step, and the tool automatically rewords and restates it in the conclusion.
  • 🔁 Auto-Populated Body Paragraphs: Student reasons and evidence are carried forward into structured paragraphs.
  • 📝 Final Essay Compilation: The tool automatically combines all parts into a full draft—ready for feedback, revision, or export.

No more copy-paste frustration—just seamless, structured writing from start to finish.

🧑‍💻 Writing Coach

This built-in coach provides instant feedback to help students catch and fix common writing issues as they go:

  • 🔍 Grammar & Mechanics: Highlights errors like sentence fragments, run-ons, first and second-person pronouns, contractions, informal language and double negatives—encouraging clean, academic writing.
  • 💬 Clarity & Style: Detects off-topic responses, repetition, filler words, and awkward phrasing—pushing students to think clearly and revise with intention.
  • 🔗 Structure Support: Flags missing transitions, missing quotation marks, weak evidence explanations, and structure issues without ever writing the content for the student.

Students get specific, supportive guidance—building stronger, more independent writers with every section.

🛡️ Academic Integrity Checks

This tool includes features designed to support authentic student work and discourage misuse of AI:

  • 📜 Edit History Logs: Tracks writing sessions and timestamps for each section, making it easier to spot copying, pasting, or AI-assisted writing.
  • 🕵️ Teacher Oversight: Helps teachers monitor progress and verify that students are engaging meaningfully with their writing tasks.
  • ✅ Encourages Authenticity: Creates a transparent writing process that rewards effort, revision, and growth over shortcuts.

Built with accountability in mind, this feature promotes honest effort and helps uphold academic standards in any classroom setting.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

🧾 Do students need accounts?

Nope! Just share the organizer link and access code. Students can start writing instantly.

🏫 Can I use this with Google Classroom?

Yes! Simply post the link and code for your students to use. No logins or integrations needed.

🗓️ How long does access last?

Your license is valid for one full school year. You'll receive all updates during that time, and new access codes will be issued each August.

🔐 Is student data collected or stored?

No student data is ever collected or stored. This tool runs entirely in the browser, meaning all writing stays on the student’s device. No accounts, logins, or emails are required, and nothing is sent to a server. It’s fully FERPA- and GDPR-compliant and safe for classroom use.
